Peanut Butter Overnight Oats

Description

A high protein overnight oats including: Milk, Peanut Butter, \ Protein Powder, Rolled Oats, Maple Syrup.

Ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Oats
  • Protein Powder
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

Steps

  1. Combine wet ingredients. Whisk the syrup, peanut butter and milk together in a bowl.
  2. Add the protein powder. Stir in the protein powder until it's dissolved.
    3. \
  3. Add oats.The last step is to stir in the oats, then place the bowl in the refrigerator overnight. You also divide it into jars beforehand if you prefer.

Strawberry Banana Overnight Oats

Description

Combination of the two best fruits, topped with sweented honey.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Honey
  • Banana
  • Strawberry
    • \

Steps

  1. Place oats in a bowl and set aside
  2. Whisk milk, honey, and yogurt
  3. Pour wet ingredients over oats and stir to combine
  4. Stir strawberries and bananas
  5. Place bowl in the refrigerator
